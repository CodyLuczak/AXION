Frequently Asked Questions

Is the $5.99 a subscription?

No. The $5.99 fee is a one-time payment. You’ll get your resume and job suggestions within 24-42 hours without recurring charges.

Do I need a subscription?

No. Subscriptions are optional. They provide faster results, additional templates, and premium features, but are not required to use AXION.

How fast will I get my resume?

It depends on your plan: One-off access: 24-42h, Basic: 12-24h, Standard: 6-12h, Premium: 2-6h. Faster plans also include advanced resume options.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Start with one-off access or a lower plan and upgrade anytime to faster delivery or premium features.

Is my information safe?

Yes. All submitted data is encrypted and handled securely. Your information is only used to generate resumes and job suggestions.
