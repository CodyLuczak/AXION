Before you agree, get a second opinion.

SecondLook helps you understand important decisions before you commit. Upload a document or explain your situation and receive a clear, plain-English breakdown of what you’re really agreeing to.

What you can submit

  • Job offers
  • Contracts or agreements
  • Quotes or invoices
  • Courses or programs
  • Any decision you’re unsure about

What you’ll receive

  • A simple explanation
  • Hidden risks or downsides
  • Who it’s good (or bad) for
  • Questions you should ask
  • Clear red flags, if any
