How It Works

1. Enter Your Interests

Tell us your passions, skills, and the type of work you enjoy. Include your location to get local and global job suggestions. The more detailed your profile, the better the recommendations.

2. AI Generates Resume

Our AI analyzes your information to create a professional, polished resume that highlights your strengths and skills. You can download or customize it for each application.

3. Job Recommendations

Receive a curated list of jobs in your city or globally that match your profile. Each recommendation includes salary range, required skills, and company overview to save you research time.
